My Ventures

SAR Robotics Pvt. Ltd.
SAR Robotics Pvt. Ltd. is a software consulting and technology solutions company focused on innovation and digital transformation. The company specializes in both in-house product development and client-based software solutions. Its expertise includes web development, mobile applications, automation, AI solutions, and business software. SAR Robotics delivers customized and scalable technologies tailored to business needs. The company works with startups, brands, and enterprises across multiple industries. Driven by creativity and technology, it aims to build smart, reliable, and future-ready digital solutions.

PageantIcon
PageantIcon is a global pageantry marketplace platform dedicated to beauty, confidence, and talent. The platform connects participants, organizers, brands, and pageant enthusiasts worldwide through events and competitions. PageantIcon provides opportunities for aspiring models and beauty queens to participate in national and international pageants. It also serves as a marketplace for pageantry products, including crowns, gowns, accessories, makeup, and fashion essentials. The platform combines pageantry, fashion, and digital commerce into one ecosystem. With a vision of empowerment and glamour, PageantIcon aims to redefine the modern pageantry industry globally.

Queen by Destiny

Sudha Shakywar

"Queens are not born through comfort or privilege; they are created through struggles, values, patience, courage, and the strength to rise after every fall."
Sudha Shakywar, nick name Himani, is an Indian entrepreneur, software professional, beauty queen, and founder of multiple ventures across technology, fashion, and pageantry. Known for her inspiring journey from a middle-class upbringing to becoming a business leader and women's mentor, she believes that life itself is the greatest teacher — shaping strength, wisdom, resilience, and purpose.

Often described as a "Queen by Destiny," Sudha's life story reflects courage, transformation, and the belief that every struggle carries a lesson capable of creating greatness.
◆ Early Life and Family

Sudha Shakywar was born and raised in Indore in a humble middle-class family. Her father served as a supervisor in the Madhya Pradesh State Electricity Board, while her mother devoted her life to nurturing the family with unconditional love, sacrifice, and compassion.

Growing up with two sisters and one brother, Sudha was raised in an environment where daughters were empowered equally. Her parents never differentiated between sons and daughters and taught their children to live with dignity, confidence, and self-respect.

From an early age, Sudha learned that beauty is not defined by skin color, appearance, or social status, but by character, values, kindness, and inner strength. She often credits her parents as her first teachers and greatest inspirations.

◆ Education

With the unwavering support of her family, Sudha pursued higher education and completed her Master of Computer Applications (MCA), specializing in computer science and software development.

Her educational journey became the foundation of her confidence, independence, and professional growth.

◆ Professional Journey

Sudha began her career in the software industry, working with leading technology companies as a software professional. During this phase of her life, she met her life partner, and together they built a relationship rooted in trust, understanding, and mutual support.

After embracing motherhood, Sudha made the heartfelt decision to step away from a high-paying corporate career to focus on raising her son and fulfilling her responsibilities toward her family.

What many saw as a pause in her career became one of the most transformative chapters of her life. Life became her greatest teacher. Through responsibilities, sacrifices, emotional battles, and silent struggles, Sudha discovered new strength within herself. Instead of losing her identity, she transformed herself — balancing motherhood, ambition, dreams, and purpose.

◆ Pageantry and Fashion

Driven by her passion for fashion, confidence, and women empowerment, Sudha entered the world of beauty pageants. For her, pageantry was never only about crowns or glamour; it became a platform to inspire women to rise above limitations and societal stereotypes.

Through national and international pageants, she earned recognition for her grace, confidence, leadership, and inspiring life story. She later became associated with the pageant industry as a mentor, jury member, tourism ambassador, and guide for aspiring queens and young women seeking confidence and direction in life.

Her journey proved that a woman can be a mother, entrepreneur, leader, and queen — all at the same time.

◆ Entrepreneurship

Inspired by her experiences in technology, family life, pageantry, and leadership, Sudha ventured into entrepreneurship and co-founded multiple business initiatives:

  • SAR Robotics Pvt. Ltd.
  • Pageant Icon
  • 99S

Through these ventures, she continues to work toward innovation, empowerment, community building, and creating opportunities for aspiring talent.

◆ Personal Philosophy

Sudha Shakywar believes that every experience in life carries meaning and every challenge carries a lesson. According to her, life itself is the greatest teacher — shaping ordinary individuals into extraordinary souls.

Her journey reflects resilience, sacrifice, elegance, motherhood, ambition, and self-belief.

She believes queens are not born through comfort or privilege; they are created through struggles, values, patience, courage, and the strength to rise after every fall.

Today, Sudha stands as a symbol of grace and determination — a woman shaped by life, empowered by purpose, and truly a Queen by Destiny.
